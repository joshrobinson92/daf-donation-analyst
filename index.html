Recommend Grants

Distribute grants to your favorite 501(c)(3) charities over time on your own schedule.

📈

Immediate Tax Deductions

Get a tax deduction up to 60% of AGI for cash, or up to 30% for appreciated assets immediately. Your assets grow while you select charities over months or years.

🍀

Bypass Capital Gains

Donate long-term appreciated stock directly to your DAF. You pay 0% capital gains tax and deduct the full fair-market value, amplifying giving power by up to 20%.

⚡

Consolidated Simplicity

Ditch the clutter of charity tax receipts. Contribute once to fund your DAF, receive one tax receipt, and recommend multiple grants to different nonprofits from one central account.
